Plate2Purpose

Plate2Purpose is a student-based campus event with the aim of reducing food waste through efficient sharing, consumption, and planning.

Learn More

CircuitCycle

CircuitCycle is a campaign with the aim of reducing e-waste through the use and disposal of e-devices, as well as the responsible use of technology among IIT students.

Learn More

GoThrift 26'

GoThrift 26' is a community-led thrifting event conducted on campus with the aim of reducing textile waste through the provision of affordable fashion to the community.

Learn More

ShoreSafe

ShoreSafe is a student-led EcoLoop initiative focused on reducing plastic pollution through direct local action. .

Learn More

LastBite 2026

A fun competition, where students take part in a hot dog eating contest while raising awareness about food waste. All profits and food donations go to charity.
